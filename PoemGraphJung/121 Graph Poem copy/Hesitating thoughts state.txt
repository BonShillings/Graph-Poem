Hesitating thoughts state “Wait! Don’t take the flight.”
Meditations don’t keep hallucinations
Dissipate not; not one lie lied in your eyes
Face it, beauty, needed was the occasion.
Struggling; it’s hard to hold the elusive.
Much it is missed. That’s it: the conclusive truth.
Disaligning: dreams and real. Exclusive
they must be. A constant paradox; a feud.
Fuel of the spirit, this is it, something knows.
Shared being shows sides; Forced to wonder, wander.
Beyond knowledge, hostage, it’s honest. I’m sold.
touched, enough to off the hinge. Much to ponder.
Left soaking, knowing, loathing that I can’t have.
Yet so beautiful the stolen moment had.
